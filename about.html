About

The power of text extraction, manipulation, and analysis in an API.

What and why

TextAPI will put the power of natural language processing into the hands of the everyday computer programmer or industrious business owner.

It is surfacing this power to do text analysis, extraction, and manipulation via a language agnostic web api. This allows a developer of any walk of life whether it be php or C# to be able to leverage this potential. To see the current supported features please visit this page.

Future updates

We give priority to our paying customers for the direction and roadmap of our future updates, but suggestions are always welcome. Please email support@textapi.com with any suggestions as we are always happy to hear them.
